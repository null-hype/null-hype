# null-hype ($H_0$)

> Well, obviously it just looks as though the Sun is going round the Earth.
> *Yet Galileo understood: the burden of proof belongs to those who claim revolution.*

### 0x00: The Axiom

In science, the **Null Hypothesis ($H_0$)** assumes there is no relationship between variables until significant evidence proves otherwise. It is an act of intellectual humility—a refusal to believe in the extraordinary without testimony from the extraordinary itself.

In software, **Null Hype** builds upon this foundation:

1. Every "revolutionary" tool demands interrogation. Strip away the vendor poetry and ask: *What problem does this genuinely solve?*
2. Security is not a feature bolted on afterward—it is the architecture's first principle, the foundation upon which trust is built.
3. Systems that require constant human intervention confess their own failures. True architecture is elegant enough to run while you are elsewhere.

This profile is less a portfolio of promises and more a *testament*—a record of what survives scrutiny, what endures questioning, what remains standing when the applause fades.

### 0x02: The Philosophy

**Contingency over Truth** *(Rorty)* — There is no Platonic Form of perfect code hovering in the heavens. There is only the code that speaks to *this moment*, to *this particular human conversation* about what problems matter now. This is not pessimism; it is liberation. We are freed from the tyranny of absolutes.

**Panta Rhei** *(Heraclitus)* — You cannot step into the same `main` branch twice. The river of change flows beneath every system we build. And yet, we refuse to drown in that river. Immutable infrastructure is our reply to impermanence—a way of saying: *Yes, all things flow, and therefore we shall build things that do not fear their own transformation.*

**Kein Hype, Nur Daten** *(German)* — In a world drunk on narrative, we choose sobriety. We traffic in evidence, in measurable things, in what remains true when the poetry is stripped away. Not because data is more beautiful than dreams—but because dreams without evidence are merely fantasies.
